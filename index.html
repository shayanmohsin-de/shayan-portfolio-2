I'm a Data Engineer who's obsessed with the craft of building data systems that are fast, reliable, and self-healing. I've worked across startups and mid-scale companies, designing everything from real-time streaming pipelines to petabyte-scale data lakes.

My philosophy: data infrastructure should be invisible. If stakeholders are thinking about pipelines, something's wrong. I build systems that just work — and alert me before users notice.
